A very common outdoor activity is to go camping. This invloves plenty of specific supplies, such as a tent, backpack, and fire starting materials like matches and a flamable material like lint. Also important is a sleeping bag/cot and a first aid kit for emergencies.

Some equipment that may not be imediatly thought of can include a tarp. If one is not included with the tent, it is important to bring one in case of unexpected rain. It can also be beneficial to bring a rope. This can be used in a number of ways from clothslines to simple structures like tripods.
