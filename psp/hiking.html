When hiking on a trail, it is very important to be prepared. Without proper preparation, one can easily risk exhaustion or more severe outcomes such as heat-stroke. As such, it is very important to bring plenty of water and dress suitably for whatever weather you will be dealing with.

You should make sure to be familiar with the terrain you will be working with beforehand and be sure to bring any equipment that may be helpful, such as a hiking stick, climbing boots, etc. You should also be sure to bring a phone of some kind in case of emergency. Without one it would be very hard to get help if injured or stuck in any way.
